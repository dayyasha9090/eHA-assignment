Meatpie

Nigerian Meat Pie is my go-to Nigerian pastry. I always make sure I’ve got them stored away in my freezer so I can easily reach for them whenever I begin to crave junk food. Nigerian meat pie is quite easy to make, you can even use the same recipe for Chicken pie, just replace the minced meat with minced chicken and you’re good to go. I thought I loved my Meat Pie plain and simple until I got to speak with someone recently who wanted me to make meat pies for him but he wanted just the mince and potatoes in them, nothing more. No carrots, no peppers, no onions, nothing! Just the mince and potatoes, different strokes ei? Truth be told I’m not a fan of “crowded” meat pies as well, I love to keep them as simple as possible.
